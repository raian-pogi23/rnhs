ICT

The ICT strand stands for "Information and Communication Technology". It is a specialization under the TVL track in the Senior High School (SHS) program in RNHS that aims to provide students with the knowledge and skills to use technology effectively and efficiently in various fields.

Students who choose the ICT strand will take subjects that cover different aspects of information and communication technology such as computer hardware and software, networking, web development, digital animation, and multimedia production, among others. They will also have opportunities for on-the-job training, industry immersion, and certification exams that will help them acquire the necessary skills and competencies that are required by various industries and employers.

After completing the ICT strand, students can pursue further studies in related fields such as computer science, information technology, and multimedia arts. They can also work in various industries such as software development, IT support, web design and development, animation, and game development, among others.
