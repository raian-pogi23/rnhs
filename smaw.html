SMAW

SMAW stands for "Shielded Metal Arc Welding". It is a specialization under the TVL track in the Senior High School (SHS) program in the Philippines that focuses on providing students with practical skills and knowledge in welding.

Students who choose the SMAW strand will take subjects that cover different aspects of welding such as metal fabrication, welding theory, welding safety, welding inspection, and testing procedures, among others. They will also have opportunities for on-the-job training, industry immersion, and certification exams that will help them acquire the necessary skills and competencies that are required by various industries and employers.

After completing the SMAW strand, students can pursue further studies in related fields such as welding engineering and technology. They can also work in various industries such as construction, manufacturing, automotive, and aerospace, among others, where welding is a critical skill that is required for various tasks such as repairing, fabricating, and assembling metal structures and components.
