TVL

TVL stands for "Technical-Vocational-Livelihood". It is a strand under the Senior High School (SHS) program at RNHS that aims to provide students with practical skills and competencies that are relevant to various industries and vocations.

Students who choose TVL as their strand will take subjects that are focused on developing technical and vocational skills in specific areas such as culinary arts, automotive technology, electrical installation and maintenance, and computer hardware servicing, among others. They will also have opportunities for on-the-job training, industry immersion, and certification exams that will help them acquire the necessary skills and competencies that are required by various industries and employers.

After completing the TVL strand, students can pursue further studies in technical-vocational courses or enroll in higher education programs that are related to their chosen field. Alternatively, they can also start their own businesses or work in various industries such as food and beverage, automotive, construction, information technology, and other skilled trades.
