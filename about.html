About RNHS

Rosales National High School (RNHS) is a public high school located in Rosales, Pangasinan, in the northern part of the Philippines. It was established in 1908 and has since then provided quality education to students from Rosales and neighboring towns.

RNHS offers both junior high school and senior high school programs, which cater to students from Grades 7 to 12. The school offers various academic tracks in the senior high school program, including the Academic Track (ABM, HUMSS, STEM), Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) Track, and General Academic Strand (GAS).

The school also offers a range of extracurricular activities and clubs, such as sports teams, music and arts clubs, and academic organizations, to help students develop their talents and interests.

RNHS prides itself on its commitment to academic excellence and its holistic approach to education, which aims to produce well-rounded individuals who are equipped with the knowledge and skills to succeed in their chosen careers and in life.
