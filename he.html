HE

HE stands for "Home Economics". It is a specialization under the TVL track in the Senior High School (SHS) program in the Philippines that aims to provide students with practical skills and knowledge in managing households, food production, and hospitality.

Students who choose the HE strand will take subjects that cover different aspects of home economics such as food and beverage services, tourism, hospitality management, dressmaking, handicrafts, and household management, among others. They will also have opportunities for on-the-job training, industry immersion, and certification exams that will help them acquire the necessary skills and competencies that are required by various industries and employers.

After completing the HE strand, students can pursue further studies in related fields such as culinary arts, hospitality management, fashion design, and tourism. They can also work in various industries such as food service, hotels and resorts, fashion, and handicrafts, among others, where their skills and knowledge in home economics are highly valued.
